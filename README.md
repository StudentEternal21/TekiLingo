# TekoLingo
## Description 
>[!error]
>The *problem statement* that my team chose is **Digital Literacy**. Our app is going to be a **mobile app** similar to Brilliant and Duolingo. The goal is to gamify learning simple digital concepts like cybersecurity, operating system structure, and simple digital concepts.

>[!note] Goal
> Our **objective** is to help people with low digital literacy learn through simple, interactive, and fun games; teaching users important and recent technologies and its dangers in a fun way. 

- ***Here are some key features***: 
	- **Practical Sessions**: The curriculum is broken into small, practical, and highly relevant units, focusing on ***essential skills like secure online banking***, safe communication (video calls), and common scam defense. 
	- **AI Companion**: A critical support tool providing a patient, non-judgmental digital mentor accessible through chat or **voice chat**. This companion speaks clearly and slowly, helping users get through tougher lessons and even help them practice what they learn. Low-
	- **Stress Games**: The platform uses short, engaging games designed to test practical judgment rather than speed. **Examples include simulators for spotting fraudulent emails and exercises for recognizing AI-generated content (deepfakes/bots).**
